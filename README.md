# 📊 Retail Sales Analytics & Customer Behavior Analysis using Python

A comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project performed on a retail sales dataset to uncover customer purchasing behavior, sales trends, and business insights using Python.

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales transaction data to identify:

- Customer demographics and buying patterns
- Top-performing states and regions
- Revenue contribution by occupation and gender
- Product category performance
- Sales trends and business opportunities

The analysis helps businesses make data-driven decisions for customer targeting, inventory planning, and marketing strategies.

---

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

✔️ Married women aged 26–35 are among the highest spending customer segments.

✔️ Customers from Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh contribute significantly to overall sales.

✔️ IT, Healthcare, and Aviation professionals generate substantial revenue.

✔️ Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories drive the majority of sales.

✔️ Targeted marketing campaigns can be designed based on demographic purchasing patterns.
